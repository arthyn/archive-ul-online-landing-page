Get your degree today!

The Online Bachelor of General Studies curriculum is designed to help you get the most out of your education at UL Lafayette—without even setting foot on campus. You can take courses in most of the University's academic departments and colleges, shaping and customizing your curriculum to fit your needs and aspirations.

Requirements

To be eligible for program entry, you must:

  • Have at least 45 college credit hours completed.
  • Meet the minimum GPA requirements:
    • 2.0 GPA for re-entering UL Lafayette students
    • 2.25 GPA for transfer students

Flexibility

Through our 100% online program, students have the flexibility they need to complete their bachelor's degree—on their own time and terms. Because of the freedom it offers, this program is ideal for working professionals and full-time parents. Each curriculum is completely unique and tailored to fit the student’s individual needs.

Working with program mentors, you will have the opportunity to:

  • customize your personal course path.
  • take classes from a wide range of disciplines.
  • complete concentrations in Arts & Humanities, Behavioral Science or Applied Sciences.
  • attain education milestones that speak to your specific interests and goals.
  • develop professional skills to excel in your career.

Value

There’s a sense of pride and accomplishment that comes with earning a college degree. We know the career opportunities a college degree creates for those who hold one. We also know that sometimes life happens.

Plans can change, and paths will shift. That’s why our online program is here to meet you wherever you are—on your terms and at your pace. Program mentors will work with you one-on-one to ensure your degree curriculum is both personally and professionally enriching.

Investment

Education is one of the most important financial and personal investments you’ll make. However, time and money become obstacles for many students and often hinder the road to graduation.

Through the online General Studies program, we aim to help minimize the effects of these obstacles. Our team of mentors works with each student to plan a path that fits their unique time and financial restraints.

The first step to finishing your degree starts here. To request more information on the program, please complete the form provided.
